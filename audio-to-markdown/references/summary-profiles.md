# Summary Profiles

## General

Use for lectures, personal notes, interviews, research recordings, and unknown
content types.

Sections:

- Summary
- Key Points
- Recommendations
- Action Items
- Risks And Open Questions
- Quality Notes

## Meeting

Use when the request mentions meeting notes, minutes, decisions, action items,
participants, agenda, follow-up, or stakeholder discussion.

Sections:

- Meeting Summary
- Participants
- Decisions
- Discussion Notes
- Action Items
- Recommendations
- Risks And Open Questions
- Quality Notes

Action items should include owner, timestamp, evidence, and confidence. Use
`Unclear` or `Not specified` rather than inventing missing details.

## Interview

Use when the request names an interview, Q&A, candidate screen, customer call, or
oral history.

Sections:

- Interview Summary
- Participants
- Themes
- Notable Quotes
- Follow-ups
- Quality Notes

Quote only short excerpts and avoid long verbatim passages.

## Lecture

Use for classes, talks, webinars, and tutorials.

Sections:

- Lecture Summary
- Concepts
- Examples
- Takeaways
- Questions To Review
- Quality Notes

## Research

Use for lab notes, paper discussions, experiment updates, and technical research
meetings.

Sections:

- Research Summary
- Claims And Evidence
- Methods Or Experiments
- Findings
- Open Questions
- Next Steps
- Quality Notes

## Research Meeting

Use for meetings with a supervisor, advisor, professor, thesis committee, or
research mentor. Prefer this profile when the recording is mainly critique,
revision guidance, or preparation for the next research milestone.

Sections:

- Research Meeting Summary
- Advisor Questions
- Required Revisions
- Weak Points Raised
- Unanswered Questions
- Next Actions
- Next Meeting Checklist
- Evidence-Based Action Items
- Quality Notes

Action items must include timestamp, evidence sentence, inferred owner when
possible, and confidence. Treat diarization-free owner labels as inference, not
fact.

If the quality status is `failed_stt_quality_gate`, skip these analysis sections
and render only the failure report.
